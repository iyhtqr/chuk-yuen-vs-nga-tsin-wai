Burra Charter-Inspired Preservation Principle

Cultural Significance (Authenticity + Integrity) × Understanding First × Adaptive Management = Retention

Chuk Yuen (1957): ❌ Low integrity + early irreversible loss + rigid clearance = Demolition in 2026
Nga Tsin Wai (ongoing): ✅ Retained authenticity & integrity (Grade III relics + AMO archaeology) + prolonged understanding process (including private developer delay) + URA adaptive approach = Core preserved as Conservation Park

Inspired by The Burra Charter (Australia ICOMOS, 2013): Conservation succeeds when cultural significance is first understood and assessed holistically (via authenticity & integrity), before decisions are made, and actions allow compatible change while retaining core values.
